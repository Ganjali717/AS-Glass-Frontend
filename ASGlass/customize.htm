Edging: Flat Polish

Seamed Edge


Seamed edges are lightly sanded so that the edges aren't sharp and the glass is safe to handle. Choose a seamed edge if the glass will be framed or the edges won't be visible.

Flat Polish Edge


Flat polished edges are safe and smooth with a very small 45° angle on the top and bottom edges of the glass. Choose flat polished edges when the glass edges will be visible and you desire a minimalistic look.

Pencil Polish Edge


This edge type is smooth and rounded like a pencil or “C” shape. Choose pencil polish edges if you want a more modern finish for a table top or visible glass edge.

Beveled Polish Edge


Beveled edges are polished at an angle, sloping downward towards the edge of the glass. Choose a beveled edge for an attractive yet traditional look for your glass edges. Edges can be beveled from ¼” to 1-¾”, depending on your preference.
